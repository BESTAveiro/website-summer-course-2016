Beesweet suports Summer Course

August 21, 2016 at 00:51 AM


Beesweet is one of BEST Aveiro’ partners for the summer course “The engine beneath your skin!” with the subject biomechanics and medical devices.
This brand of flavoured honey, arouses from the passion of two young cousins and the dream to bring to the market an innovative product based on honey.<
It is a raw honey obtained through artisanal processes, with healthy temperatures, helping to mantain all the caractheristics of a 100% natural honey. All honey is analysed in a laboratory in order to satisfy the food safety requirements.
Being a partner in this event, Beesweet will give the oportunity to 25 european students to taste an innovative artisanal product in the portuguese market, offering flavoured honey jars.
For more information about Beesweet: http://beesweet.eu/
